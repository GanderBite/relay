# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In brief: be welcoming, respectful, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at the email listed in `package.json`. Reports will be reviewed and responded to within 7 days.
